A comprehensive family healthcare ministry of the Anglican faith.

Who We Are

We are an Anglican-founded, humane, faith-based organisation.We believe that excellent health is a gift from God, and that everyone, regardless of background or circumstance, is entitled to adequate healthcare. Our team is made up of committed medical experts and volunteers that have a love for helping underprivileged families.

What we do

Our healthcare services for families encompass a broad spectrum, which includes:

  • Preventing health problems routine vaccinations,Screenings, and examinations such well-woman exams, pregnancy screenings, childbirth education classes, lactation support groups.
  • Therapy for common ailments and wounds including respiratory infections, earaches, minor cuts, or sprains.
  • Care throughout and after pregnancy.
  • Nutritional guidance as well as health instruction.
  • Spiritual counselling and pastoral care.

Our goal

Our ultimate goal is to help families enjoy healthier and more happy lives. In order to do this, we

  • Take down obstacles to receiving healthcare.
  • Supplying information and tools to encourage healthy living.
  • Creating a community that is encouraging so that families might prosper.

Our Objectives:

  • To improve underprivileged families' access to high-quality healthcare services.
  • Encourage the adoption of healthy lifestyles and preventative care.
  • To provide compassionate support and spiritual advice to families dealing with health issues.
